SETTLEMENT AND RELEASE AGREEMENT

This Settlement and Release Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into as of
May 22, 2024 (the "Effective Date") by and between:

  CLAIMANT: Hawthorne Biologics Inc., a Delaware corporation with its principal
  place of business at 88 Cambridge Park Drive, Cambridge, MA 02140 ("Claimant");
  and

  RESPONDENT: NorthernPath Manufacturing GmbH, a Bavarian limited liability
  company with its principal place of business at Maximilianstrasse 12,
  80539 Munich, Germany ("Respondent").

Claimant and Respondent are referred to individually as a "Party" and
collectively as the "Parties."

WHEREAS, on October 4, 2023, Claimant initiated arbitration proceedings under
the rules of the International Chamber of Commerce (ICC Case No. 28114/MK)
alleging breach of the parties' Manufacturing Supply Agreement dated March 1,
2022 (the "MSA"); and

WHEREAS, the Parties have, after extensive negotiation, agreed to settle all
claims, counterclaims, and disputes arising out of or relating to the MSA and
the arbitration on the terms set forth below;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants set forth herein, the
Parties agree as follows:

1. SETTLEMENT PAYMENT. Respondent shall pay Claimant the sum of EUR 245,000
   (Two Hundred Forty-Five Thousand Euros) in full and final settlement of all
   claims (the "Settlement Payment"). The Settlement Payment shall be made by
   wire transfer to Claimant's designated account no later than thirty (30)
   days after the Effective Date.

2. KEY COMMERCIAL TERMS. The Parties have agreed to the following commercial
   terms as part of the consideration for this Agreement:

   a) Exclusivity. Respondent shall not, for a period of three (3) years from
      the Effective Date, supply identical or substantially similar product
      lines to any of the named competitors of Claimant listed on Schedule A.

   b) Indemnification cap. Respondent's aggregate indemnification liability
      under any successor supply arrangement between the Parties shall not
      exceed two times (2x) the trailing-twelve-month purchase volume,
      capped at EUR 1,000,000.

   c) Cure-period clause. Either Party shall have a right to cure any
      alleged material breach of any successor supply arrangement within
      forty-five (45) days of written notice from the non-breaching Party.
      The Parties expressly agree that this 45-day cure period replaces the
      30-day cure period in the prior MSA.

   d) Governing law and venue. This Agreement and any successor supply
      arrangement shall be governed by the laws of Switzerland; disputes
      shall be resolved exclusively by ICC arbitration in Geneva.

3. MUTUAL RELEASES. Effective upon Claimant's receipt of the Settlement
   Payment, each Party releases the other from all claims, known or unknown,
   arising out of or relating to the MSA, the arbitration, or the conduct
   underlying either, except for the obligations expressly set forth in this
   Agreement.

4. WITHDRAWAL OF ARBITRATION. Within five (5) business days of Claimant's
   receipt of the Settlement Payment, the Parties shall jointly request that
   the arbitral tribunal terminate the arbitration with prejudice and without
   any award of costs.

5. CONFIDENTIALITY. The existence and terms of this Agreement are confidential
   and shall not be disclosed by either Party except (i) as required by law or
   regulation, (ii) to professional advisors under a duty of confidence, or
   (iii) with the prior written consent of the other Party.

6. ENTIRE AGREEMENT. This Agreement constitutes the entire understanding of
   the Parties with respect to its subject matter and supersedes all prior
   negotiations, representations, and agreements.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Parties have executed this Agreement as of the
Effective Date.
