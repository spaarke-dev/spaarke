SETTLEMENT AGREEMENT AND MUTUAL RELEASE

This Settlement Agreement and Mutual Release ("Agreement") is entered into as of
August 15, 2024 (the "Effective Date") by and between Acme Corporation, a
Delaware corporation with its principal place of business at 1500 Industrial
Parkway, Wilmington, DE ("Acme"), and Northstar Holdings LLC, a Nevada limited
liability company with its principal place of business at 200 Range Road,
Reno, NV ("Northstar"). Acme and Northstar are each a "Party" and collectively
the "Parties."

RECITALS

WHEREAS, on January 14, 2024, Acme filed a complaint in the United States
District Court for the District of Delaware, Civil Action No. 24-cv-00118-RGA,
asserting claims against Northstar including breach of the parties' April 2022
Patent License Agreement (the "License"), tortious interference, and unjust
enrichment (collectively, the "Litigation");

WHEREAS, the Parties desire to fully and finally resolve all claims and
counterclaims raised in the Litigation without further proceedings and without
admission of liability;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants set forth herein and
other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are
hereby acknowledged, the Parties agree as follows:

1. SETTLEMENT PAYMENT.

   Northstar shall pay to Acme the total sum of Three Hundred Ten Thousand
   United States Dollars ($310,000 USD) (the "Settlement Amount") by wire
   transfer to Acme's designated account within thirty (30) calendar days
   following the Effective Date.

2. MUTUAL RELEASE.

   Effective upon receipt of the Settlement Amount, each Party, on behalf of
   itself and its affiliates, officers, directors, employees, agents, successors,
   and assigns, hereby releases, acquits, and forever discharges the other Party
   and its affiliates, officers, directors, employees, agents, successors, and
   assigns from any and all claims, demands, actions, and causes of action,
   whether known or unknown, arising out of or related to the Litigation or the
   License through the Effective Date.

3. CURE PERIOD.

   In the event of any alleged breach of this Agreement by either Party, the
   non-breaching Party shall provide written notice of such alleged breach and
   the breaching Party shall have forty-five (45) days from receipt of such
   notice to cure (the "Cure Period"). No action for breach may be commenced
   until the Cure Period has expired without cure.

4. CONFIDENTIALITY AND NON-DISPARAGEMENT.

   The Parties agree that the existence and terms of this Agreement shall be
   confidential, except as required by applicable law or for accounting,
   regulatory, or tax purposes. Neither Party shall make any disparaging
   statements regarding the other Party for a period of three (3) years from
   the Effective Date.

5. NO ADMISSION OF LIABILITY.

   This Agreement is a compromise of disputed claims and shall not be construed
   as an admission of liability by either Party.

6. GOVERNING LAW; ENTIRE AGREEMENT.

   This Agreement shall be governed by Delaware law. This Agreement constitutes
   the entire agreement between the Parties with respect to the subject matter
   hereof and supersedes all prior negotiations.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Parties have executed this Agreement as of the
Effective Date.
