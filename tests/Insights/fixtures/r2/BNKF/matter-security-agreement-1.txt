SECURITY AGREEMENT

This Security Agreement (this "Security Agreement"), dated as of February 27,
2026, is by and among Mariner Logistics Group LLC, a Delaware limited
liability company, and Mariner Coastal Operations LLC, a Virginia limited
liability company (each a "Grantor" and collectively, the "Grantors"), in
favor of Linden Trust National Bank, N.A., as administrative agent for the
Secured Parties (in such capacity, the "Administrative Agent").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Grantors, the Administrative Agent, and the Lenders are entering
into that certain Credit and Security Agreement dated as of even date herewith
(as amended, restated, supplemented, or otherwise modified from time to time,
the "Credit Agreement"); and

WHEREAS, as a condition precedent to the Lenders' obligation to extend credit
under the Credit Agreement, the Grantors are required to grant to the
Administrative Agent, for the ratable benefit of the Secured Parties, a
security interest in the Collateral described herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the foregoing and other good and valuable
consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which are hereby acknowledged,
the Grantors hereby agree as follows:

SECTION 1. DEFINITIONS.

Capitalized terms used but not otherwise defined herein have the meanings
ascribed to them in the Credit Agreement.

   "Collateral" means all of each Grantor's right, title, and interest in and
   to all of the following property, whether now owned or hereafter acquired
   and wherever located: (a) all Accounts; (b) all Inventory; (c) all
   Equipment; (d) all General Intangibles; (e) all Deposit Accounts and
   Securities Accounts; (f) all Investment Property; (g) all books, records,
   and documents related to any of the foregoing; and (h) all Proceeds and
   products of any of the foregoing.

   "Excluded Property" means: (i) the deposit account maintained at
   Norfolk Community Credit Union, Account No. ending in 8841, used
   exclusively as a payroll funding account; (ii) any vehicle or other
   property subject to a certificate of title, to the extent perfection of a
   security interest therein would require notation on such certificate;
   and (iii) any rights in any contract or License to the extent the grant
   of a security interest therein would result in the breach of such
   contract or License under applicable law (other than to the extent such
   prohibition is rendered ineffective under the UCC or other applicable law).

SECTION 2. GRANT OF SECURITY INTEREST.

Each Grantor hereby grants to the Administrative Agent, for the ratable
benefit of the Secured Parties, a continuing first-priority security interest
in all of such Grantor's right, title, and interest in and to the Collateral.

SECTION 3. PERFECTION.

3.1 UCC Filings. Each Grantor authorizes the Administrative Agent to file UCC
    financing statements in any jurisdiction necessary to perfect the security
    interest granted hereunder, describing the Collateral as "all assets of
    Debtor, whether now owned or hereafter acquired."

3.2 Control Agreements. Each Grantor shall use commercially reasonable efforts
    to deliver, within sixty (60) days of the Closing Date, a Deposit Account
    Control Agreement (DACA) for each Deposit Account constituting Collateral
    other than Excluded Property.

3.3 Intellectual Property Filings. Each Grantor shall execute and deliver a
    short-form Patent Security Agreement, Trademark Security Agreement, and
    Copyright Security Agreement, as applicable, for filing with the United
    States Patent and Trademark Office and the United States Copyright Office
    within fifteen (15) Business Days of the Closing Date.

SECTION 4. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES.

Each Grantor represents and warrants that: (a) it has good and marketable
title to the Collateral, free and clear of all Liens other than Permitted
Liens; (b) no UCC financing statement covering the Collateral is on file in
any jurisdiction except those in favor of the Administrative Agent;
(c) Schedule 4(c) sets forth (i) each Grantor's chief executive office,
(ii) each location at which Equipment having a value in excess of Two Hundred
Fifty Thousand Dollars ($250,000) is maintained, and (iii) each Deposit
Account and Securities Account maintained by such Grantor.

SECTION 5. COVENANTS.

5.1 Maintenance of Collateral. Each Grantor shall maintain the Collateral in
    good operating condition, ordinary wear and tear excepted, and shall
    promptly repair or replace any material Collateral that becomes damaged or
    destroyed.

5.2 Insurance. Each Grantor shall maintain insurance on the Collateral in
    accordance with Section 6.5 of the Credit Agreement, with the
    Administrative Agent named as additional insured and loss payee.

5.3 Notice of Adverse Events. Each Grantor shall give the Administrative Agent
    prompt written notice (and in any event within five (5) Business Days) of
    any (a) loss, theft, or damage to Collateral with a value in excess of
    Five Hundred Thousand Dollars ($500,000); (b) judicial seizure or
    forfeiture proceeding affecting the Collateral; or (c) change in such
    Grantor's chief executive office, name, or jurisdiction of organization.

SECTION 6. REMEDIES UPON DEFAULT.

Upon the occurrence and during the continuance of an Event of Default, the
Administrative Agent may, without further notice (except as required by law):

   (a) declare all Obligations immediately due and payable;
   (b) take possession of the Collateral and exercise all rights of a secured
       party under the UCC, including the right to sell, lease, or otherwise
       dispose of the Collateral at public or private sale;
   (c) collect, receive, and apply all Proceeds of the Collateral to the
       Obligations in such order as the Administrative Agent determines; and
   (d) exercise all other rights and remedies available at law or in equity.

SECTION 7. INDEMNIFICATION.

Each Grantor agrees to indemnify and hold harmless the Administrative Agent and
each Secured Party from and against all losses, claims, damages, liabilities,
and expenses (including reasonable attorneys' fees) arising out of or related
to the Collateral or this Security Agreement, except to the extent caused by
the gross negligence or willful misconduct of the indemnified party.

SECTION 8. GOVERNING LAW; JURISDICTION.

This Security Agreement shall be governed by New York law. The parties consent
to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts located in New York County.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the Grantors have executed this Security Agreement as of
the date first written above.
