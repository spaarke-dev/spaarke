Thank you for engaging Holwell, Strand & Adichie LLP ("HSA," "we" or "us") to
serve as Mariner Logistics Group LLC's ("Mariner" or "you") borrower's counsel
in connection with the negotiation, documentation, and closing of a senior
secured credit facility (the "Engagement"). This letter sets forth the terms
of our representation.

1. SCOPE OF ENGAGEMENT

   1.1 The Engagement covers:

       (a) negotiation of a Commitment Letter with the lead arranger and
           administrative agent (Linden Trust National Bank, N.A.) and the
           syndicate of three additional lenders identified in the
           preliminary syndication memorandum dated January 28, 2026;

       (b) negotiation and drafting of the principal loan documents,
           including (i) the Credit and Security Agreement, (ii) the
           Security Agreement, (iii) Guaranty Agreements from Mariner
           Holdings, Inc. and Mariner Coastal Operations LLC, (iv) the
           Mortgage on the Norfolk Facility, (v) the Intercreditor Agreement
           (if any), and (vi) ancillary perfection and closing documents;

       (c) the closing process, including delivery of customary borrower
           and guarantor legal opinions, satisfaction of closing conditions,
           and coordination of payoff and lien-release activities for the
           Existing Credit Facility being refinanced; and

       (d) the post-closing recordation and perfection follow-up (UCC
           continuation tickler, IP security agreement filings, mortgage
           recordation in the Hampton Roads Recorder's Office, and DACA
           coordination).

   1.2 The Engagement does NOT cover:

       (a) representation of Mariner in lender-side communications regarding
           the Existing Credit Facility (handled by Mariner's existing
           lender counsel of record);

       (b) representation of any individual employee, officer, or
           guarantor in their individual capacity, other than the corporate
           Guarantors named above;

       (c) tax structuring or tax advice — those services have been
           separately engaged with Brennan Tax LLP under that firm's
           engagement letter dated January 30, 2026; and

       (d) environmental due diligence — Penobscot & Reeves LLP has been
           engaged for that work.

3. FEE STRUCTURE

   3.1 The Engagement shall be billed on a Time-and-Materials basis at
       the firm's customary 2026 hourly rates, with a Project Budget Cap
       of THREE HUNDRED EIGHTY-FIVE THOUSAND DOLLARS ($385,000) for the
       full scope described in Section 1.1.

   3.2 If projected total fees will exceed the Budget Cap by more than
       five percent (5%), HSA will provide written notice and the
       parties will negotiate an amended budget before any work proceeds
       in excess of the Cap.

   3.3 Out-of-pocket expenses (recording fees, search and filing charges,
       overnight courier, travel) will be billed at cost and are not
       counted toward the Budget Cap. Estimated total expenses: $42,000.
