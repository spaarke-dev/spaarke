1. PROJECT OVERVIEW

This Statement of Work ("SOW") sets forth the scope, schedule, and budgetary
parameters for Greene & Marlow LLP's representation of Verdant Solar Holdings,
Inc. ("Verdant Solar") in the sale of substantially all of its operating assets
to Pelican Bay Energy Partners LLC under an Asset Purchase Agreement
contemplated to be signed February 11, 2026 and closed by April 17, 2026.

2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

   2.1 Pre-Signing Phase (Estimated: 320 hours)
       - Diligence response coordination across Verdant Solar's IP, environmental,
         and tax workstreams
       - APA drafting and counter-proposal cycles
       - Disclosure schedules compilation and review
       - Antitrust analysis (HSR threshold analysis completed: filing required;
         coordination with antitrust counsel at Holwell, Strand & Adichie LLP)

   2.2 Signing-to-Closing Phase (Estimated: 480 hours)
       - Regulatory filings and clearance (HSR, FERC notification)
       - Bring-down diligence and refresh of disclosure schedules
       - Closing logistics and document coordination
       - Negotiation of transition services arrangements (separate TSA)

   2.3 Post-Closing Phase (Estimated: 180 hours)
       - Post-closing adjustments and true-up of Working Capital
       - Indemnity claim management (12-month escrow administration)
       - Defense and resolution of any post-closing disputes
       - File transition and matter wrap-up

5. BUDGET AND BILLING APPROACH

   This project is billed on a Time-and-Materials basis with the Budget Cap
   referenced above. The Project Manager shall report budget utilization
   monthly to the Client of Record. If projected total fees will exceed the
   Budget Cap, the Project Manager shall provide written notice not less than
   thirty (30) days prior to the projected exhaustion of the Budget Cap, and
   the parties shall negotiate an amended scope or fee arrangement before any
   work proceeds in excess of the Budget Cap.

6. ASSUMPTIONS AND CONSTRAINTS

   - Verdant Solar's diligence response team will deliver documents into the
     diligence VDR within five (5) Business Days of each request.
   - No more than two (2) rounds of substantive APA negotiation are anticipated.
   - If a third-party challenge to HSR clearance materializes, this SOW will
     be paused and a separate SOW addendum negotiated.
   - The fee estimate assumes no concurrent securities-class-action exposure
     related to the announcement of the transaction.

7. DEPENDENCIES AND RELATED MATTERS

   - Linked matter CMRCL-407211 carries the substantive legal record.
   - This project depends on coordination with Brightwater Capital Advisors LLC,
     which holds a 17% minority equity interest in Verdant Solar.
   - Coordination with environmental counsel (Penobscot & Reeves LLP) is
     required for diligence of the Verdant Solar manufacturing site at
     Wilmington, DE.

8. PROJECT STATUS REPORTING
