4. CARRY-FORWARD ITEMS (TRANSITION TO NEW PROJECT)

A new project code (CTRNS-2026-074, "Verdant Solar — Post-Close Escrow
Administration") has been opened to track the following 12-month
carry-forward activities:

   a) Working Capital true-up determination (target: July 17, 2026)
   b) Defense of indemnity claims, if any, against the $3,000,000 Holdback
      Escrow held by Atlas Trust Services Inc. (escrow release date:
      April 17, 2027)
   c) Annual escrow-account audit and reporting to client
   d) Coordination with TSA counterparties during the 12-month TSA term

The estimated budget for the carry-forward project is $185,000 (T&M with
cap). A separate SOW for CTRNS-2026-074 will be issued by May 22, 2026.

5. LESSONS LEARNED

   - The Buyer-requested March 26 disclosure-refresh cycle could have been
     anticipated had the project team monitored client-side public
     announcements more actively. Recommend a public-information watch
     during pre-closing windows on future deals.
   - The Working Capital target-setting workstream slipped one week in early
     March due to client-side accounting delays. Recommend setting an
     explicit covenant in future engagement letters requiring monthly
     client-side accounting closes by a specified business day.
   - The Escrow Agreement negotiation moved more efficiently than past
     deals because Atlas Trust Services Inc. agreed to use the firm's form
     escrow agreement as the starting point. Recommend Atlas as a preferred
     escrow agent for future Greene & Marlow transactional engagements.

6. CLIENT FEEDBACK
