CLOSING STATEMENT

This Closing Statement (the "Statement") is delivered in connection with the
closing of the transactions contemplated by the Asset Purchase Agreement dated
March 17, 2026 (the "APA"), by and between Verdant Solar Holdings, Inc., a
Delaware corporation ("Seller"), and Pelican Bay Energy Partners LLC, a Texas
limited liability company ("Buyer"). Capitalized terms used and not otherwise
defined herein have the meanings ascribed to them in the APA.

CLOSING DATE: April 14, 2026
PLACE OF CLOSING: The offices of Greene & Marlow LLP, 401 South Tower, Austin, TX

1. PURCHASE PRICE.

   The aggregate Purchase Price for the Purchased Assets is Forty-Two Million
   Five Hundred Thousand United States Dollars ($42,500,000 USD), payable by
   Buyer at Closing as follows:

      (a) Cash at Closing:                 $38,750,000
      (b) Holdback Escrow (12 months):     $ 3,000,000
      (c) Working Capital True-Up Holdback: $   750,000

   The Cash at Closing portion shall be paid by wire transfer of immediately
   available funds to Seller's designated account per Schedule 1.

2. ADJUSTMENTS AT CLOSING.

   Pursuant to Section 2.7 of the APA, the Purchase Price has been adjusted as
   follows:

      Estimated Working Capital surplus:    + $   412,000
      Pre-Closing Inventory Adjustment:     - $    87,400
      Pre-Closing Capex True-Up:            + $   158,200
      Net Adjustment:                       + $   482,800

3. SETTLEMENT OF INDEBTEDNESS.

   At Closing, Seller has caused to be repaid in full all Indebtedness identified
   on Schedule 2 (the "Payoff Amount") in the aggregate amount of $8,140,000,
   funded from the Cash at Closing portion of the Purchase Price. Payoff letters
   from each holder of Indebtedness have been delivered and are attached as
   Exhibit A.

4. RELEASED LIENS.

   Upon receipt of the Payoff Amount, lenders' counsel have authorized release
   of all liens, encumbrances, and security interests in the Purchased Assets,
   and UCC termination statements have been filed in Delaware and Texas as
   listed on Schedule 3.

5. TRANSFER TAXES.

   Buyer and Seller shall share transfer taxes 50/50 pursuant to Section 8.4 of
   the APA. The aggregate transfer-tax liability is estimated at $186,400.

6. PRORATIONS.

   Real property taxes and prepaid expenses have been prorated as of the
   Closing Date in accordance with Section 2.8 of the APA. The Net Proration
   credit owed to Buyer is $24,820.

7. JURISDICTION; GOVERNING LAW.

   This Statement and any matter arising hereunder shall be governed by Delaware
   law, consistent with Section 11.6 of the APA. Any dispute concerning the
   amounts set forth herein shall be resolved exclusively in the Delaware Court
   of Chancery.

8. ESCROW AGENT; ESCROW AGREEMENT.

   The Holdback Escrow and the Working Capital True-Up Holdback shall be held
   by Atlas Trust Services Inc. pursuant to that certain Escrow Agreement of
   even date herewith (the "Escrow Agreement").

ACKNOWLEDGED AND AGREED as of the Closing Date.
