MASTER SUPPLY AGREEMENT
Version 3.2 - Effective January 1, 2026

BETWEEN:
  Spaarke Legal Services ("Buyer")
  AND
  Contoso Ltd. ("Supplier")

1. SCOPE OF AGREEMENT
This Master Supply Agreement ("Agreement") governs the supply of raw materials,
components, and finished goods from Supplier to Buyer for the fiscal year 2026.

2. PRICING AND PAYMENT TERMS
2.1 Pricing shall be as set forth in Schedule A (Pricing Schedule).
2.2 Payment terms: Net 30 from date of invoice.
2.3 Early payment discount: 2% if paid within 10 days.

3. DELIVERY
3.1 Supplier shall deliver goods FOB Destination to Buyer's designated facilities.
3.2 Standard delivery lead time: 14 business days from purchase order date.
3.3 Expedited delivery available at 15% surcharge with 5 business day lead time.

4. QUALITY STANDARDS
4.1 All goods shall conform to specifications in Schedule B.
4.2 Supplier shall maintain ISO 9001:2015 certification throughout the term.
4.3 Buyer reserves the right to conduct quality audits with 48 hours notice.

5. FORCE MAJEURE (Amended v3.2)
5.1 Neither party shall be liable for delays caused by events beyond reasonable control.
5.2 Force majeure events include but are not limited to: natural disasters, pandemics,
    government actions, supply chain disruptions, and cyber incidents.
5.3 Affected party must provide written notice within 72 hours of the event.

6. TERM AND TERMINATION
6.1 Initial term: January 1, 2026 through December 31, 2026.
6.2 Auto-renewal for successive one-year terms unless terminated with 90 days notice.
6.3 Either party may terminate for cause with 30 days written notice and cure period.

7. LIABILITY
7.1 Supplier's total liability shall not exceed the value of goods delivered in the
    preceding 12 months.
7.2 Neither party shall be liable for consequential, incidental, or punitive damages.

8. CONFIDENTIALITY
8.1 Both parties shall maintain the confidentiality of all proprietary information.
8.2 Confidentiality obligations survive termination for a period of 3 years.
