DATA PROCESSING AGREEMENT
GDPR Article 28 Compliant

Between:
  Data Controller: Spaarke Legal Services ("Controller")
  Data Processor: Northwind Traders ("Processor")

Effective Date: February 15, 2026
DPA Reference: DPA-2026-NW-003

1. DEFINITIONS
   "Personal Data" means any information relating to an identified or identifiable
   natural person as defined in GDPR Article 4(1).
   "Processing" means any operation performed on Personal Data as defined in
   GDPR Article 4(2).
   "Sub-processor" means any third party engaged by the Processor to process
   Personal Data on behalf of the Controller.

2. SUBJECT MATTER AND DURATION
   2.1 This DPA covers the processing of Personal Data in connection with the
       services provided under the Master Services Agreement dated January 2026.
   2.2 Categories of data subjects: Employees, customers, and business contacts.
   2.3 Types of personal data: Names, email addresses, phone numbers, job titles,
       transaction records, and communication logs.
   2.4 Duration: Co-terminus with the Master Services Agreement.

3. PROCESSOR OBLIGATIONS
   3.1 Process Personal Data only on documented instructions from the Controller.
   3.2 Ensure persons authorized to process data are bound by confidentiality.
   3.3 Implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures.
   3.4 Assist the Controller with data subject access requests within 72 hours.
   3.5 Delete or return all Personal Data at the end of the processing period.

4. SECURITY MEASURES
   4.1 Encryption: AES-256 for data at rest, TLS 1.3 for data in transit.
   4.2 Access controls: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication.
   4.3 Logging: All access to Personal Data logged with 90-day retention.
   4.4 Penetration testing: Annual third-party penetration test required.
   4.5 SOC 2 Type II certification maintained throughout the term.

5. SUB-PROCESSORS
   5.1 Current approved sub-processors listed in Annex B.
   5.2 Processor must notify Controller 30 days before engaging new sub-processors.
   5.3 Controller has the right to object to new sub-processors within 14 days.
   5.4 Processor remains fully liable for sub-processor compliance.

6. DATA BREACH NOTIFICATION
   6.1 Processor shall notify Controller within 24 hours of discovering a breach.
   6.2 Notification shall include: nature of breach, categories affected,
       approximate number of records, likely consequences, and measures taken.
   6.3 Processor shall cooperate fully with Controller's breach response.

7. DATA TRANSFERS
   7.1 No transfer of Personal Data outside the EEA without prior written consent.
   7.2 If transfer is approved, Standard Contractual Clauses (Module 2) shall apply.
   7.3 Transfer impact assessment required for each new destination country.

8. AUDIT RIGHTS
   8.1 Controller may audit Processor's compliance with this DPA annually.
   8.2 Processor shall provide access to relevant documentation within 10 business days.
   8.3 Audit costs borne by Controller unless non-compliance is found.
