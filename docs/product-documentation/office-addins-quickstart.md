# Spaarke Office Add-ins - Quick Start Guide

> **Version**: 1.0.0
>
> **Last Updated**: January 2026

## Overview

The Spaarke Office Add-ins let you save emails and documents directly to Spaarke DMS from within Microsoft Outlook and Word. You can also share documents from Spaarke by inserting links or attaching copies.

---

## Getting Started in 3 Steps

### Step 1: Open the Spaarke Panel

**In Outlook (Reading an email):**
- Click the **Spaarke** button in the ribbon toolbar

**In Word:**
- Click the **Spaarke** button in the Home ribbon

### Step 2: Choose What to Do

| Action | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Save to Spaarke** | Save the current email or document to Spaarke |
| **Share from Spaarke** | Insert links or attach documents from Spaarke |

### Step 3: Select Where to Save

When saving, you must choose where to file the document:

1. **Search** for a Matter, Project, Account, Contact, or Invoice
2. **Select** your choice from the results
3. Click **Save**

**Need a new record?** Use **Quick Create** to add a new Matter, Account, or Contact.

---

## Quick Reference

### Save Email to Spaarke

1. Open an email in Outlook
2. Click **Spaarke** in the ribbon
3. Choose **Save to Spaarke**
4. Select which attachments to include (if any)
5. Search and select an association (Matter, Account, etc.)
6. Click **Save**
7. Watch the progress bar - done when you see the green check

### Save Word Document to Spaarke

1. Open a document in Word
2. Click **Spaarke** in the Home ribbon
3. Choose **Save to Spaarke**
4. Search and select an association
5. Click **Save**

### Share from Spaarke (Outlook Compose)

1. While composing an email, click **Spaarke**
2. Choose **Share from Spaarke**
3. Search for the document you want to share
4. Choose:
   - **Insert Link** - Adds a clickable link to your email
   - **Attach Copy** - Attaches a copy of the file
5. For external recipients, check **Grant access** if needed
